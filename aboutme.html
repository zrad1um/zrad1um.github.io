How did I even get into development?

Wassup homies, my name is Ruslan Zhmyshenko. I am seventeen years old and from Qostanay (a city in the north of the Republic of Kazakhstan, but I will brag - I was born in the capital of the RoK, in Astana).

So far I am still in school (finishing the tenth grade), but in a year I will already be leaving its walls. As you already understood, I am a beginner (or beginning, ya fig znaet chestnoye slovo) developer and user interface designer. On this page I will tell you about myself and how I want in this direction.

In general, for most of my conscious life I wanted to be anyone but a programmer (or a developer, which is more "politically correct"). If I hadn't become interested in software engineering and website development, I would most likely have gone to study history (or law) at the Qostanay Regional University named Akhmet Baitursynuly...

I came to development quite recently - in the first half of January this year. I was talking to my homie (Alek, ahoj! Kocham cie <3) about my hardware (i3-3220, 16gb ddr3, integrated graphics (HD 2500)....) and I told him that I really wanted to become a programmer (I expressed my desire to be one back in the ninth grade, and was even going to take the state certification exam in the computer science, but my other homie dropped out of it...).

As a great homie, my elephant (and lion, and homie, etc...) supported me in this matter - that's how it all started.
